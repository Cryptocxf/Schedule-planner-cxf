## Description: <br>
Helps users plan trips, compare hotel and flight options, and produce itinerary summaries when travel, business trip, or booking needs are mentioned. Features multi-dimensional price comparison across transport modes, dates, hotels, and flights. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cryptocxf](https://clawhub.ai/user/cryptocxf) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Travelers and business users use this skill to clarify departure, destination, dates, party size, and budget, then receive hotel, transportation, and itinerary options. It is a planning aid only; users complete bookings and payments themselves. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Travel recommendations can become stale or differ from live prices, schedules, and availability. <br>
Mitigation: Use the output as planning guidance and verify prices, availability, booking terms, and schedule details on trusted travel sites before acting. <br>
Risk: Trip planning can involve sensitive travel or account information. <br>
Mitigation: Do not provide payment card numbers, account passwords, travel-account credentials, or unnecessary personal information to the assistant. <br>
